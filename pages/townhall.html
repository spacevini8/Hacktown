About Hacktown

Hacktown is a digital plot of land make for hackclubbers

It's inspired by Netizen Town and Melon town

Joining

To join, you must be a part of the Hackclub community.

If you are, message me on the HC slack (spacevini8) or (if you're feeling adventurous) fork the repo and add your plot.
